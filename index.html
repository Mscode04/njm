About Us

What is Najmul Huda ?

Najmul Huda Islamic University is a pioneering educational institution equipped with the latest specifications. It is dedicated to providing a distinguished environment for memorizing the Holy Quran and learning Islamic sciences in various fields and programs, keeping pace with developments in a manner that does not conflict with traditional values. It also strives to instill moral values and foster a spirit of religious preaching among students.

What Say Our Founder

MARHOOM - PA IBRAHIM HAJI SAHIB
FOUNDER - NAJMUL HUDA
Biography

Haji P. A. Ibrahim, born in Pallikkara, Kasaragod, to Abdullah and Aisha, was a renowned entrepreneur and philanthropist. He began his career in Dubai as a salesman in 1967 and later founded Century Trading Co LLC in partnership with the Al Shamil Group. Educated in Mangalore and Madras, his values of honesty and generosity guided him in building businesses and supporting countless charitable initiatives. He established mosques, academic institutions, and aided the underprivileged across nations. His life was a testament to service, integrity, and devotion.

Vision and Legacy

As the founder of this university, Haji P. A. Ibrahim envisioned a center of excellence that combines Islamic values with modern knowledge. He believed education is the cure for ignorance and the key to societal progress. His goal was to raise scholars who embody both faith and intellect—capable of guiding communities with wisdom and compassion. This institution stands as a reflection of his mission to serve humanity and seek Allah’s pleasure. May his legacy continue to inspire generations.
