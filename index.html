About Us

What is Najmul Huda ?

Al Jamia Njamul Huda Al Islamiya is a pioneering educational institution equipped with the latest specifications. It is dedicated to providing a distinguished environment for memorizing the Holy Quran and learning Islamic sciences in various fields and programs, keeping pace with developments in a manner that does not conflict with traditional values. It also strives to instill moral values and foster a spirit of religious preaching among students.

Marhoom P.A. Ibrahim Haji

Founder of Al Jamia Njamul Huda Al Islamiya

Details

Biography

Haji P.A. Ibrahim (1945-2020) was a visionary leader and philanthropist who dedicated his life to education and community service.

Early Life

Born in Pallikkara, Kasaragod to Abdullah and Aisha, he received his early education in Mangalore and Madras.

Career

Began as a salesman in Dubai in 1967, later founded Century Trading Co LLC with Al Shamil Group.

Philanthropy

Established mosques, academic institutions, and supported countless charitable initiatives worldwide.

Vision & Legacy

"Education is the light that dispels the darkness of ignorance and the foundation for building righteous communities."

P.A. Ibrahim Haji

  • To develop scholars who embody both faith and intellectual excellence
